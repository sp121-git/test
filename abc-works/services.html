Full Service Range

Everything Your Business Needs

Whether you're fitting out a new building or maintaining an existing system, we have you covered.

❄️ Installation Services

🏢

Corporate AC Installation

Large-scale VRF, chiller and ducted systems tailored to multi-floor offices, headquarters and business parks. We handle project management from survey to commissioning.

🛍️

Retail & Hospitality Installation

Cassette, ceiling and wall-mounted units for shops, restaurants, hotels and leisure venues. Minimal disruption to trading during installation.

🏭

Industrial & Warehouse

High-capacity evaporative coolers, split systems and precision air handling for warehouses, manufacturing plants and server rooms.

🏥

Healthcare & Education

Clean-room compliant and hygienic climate systems for hospitals, clinics, schools and universities—meeting all relevant health and safety standards.

🔧 Maintenance & Support

📅

Planned Preventive Maintenance

Annual or bi-annual PPM contracts that include filter cleaning, refrigerant checks, electrical inspections and full system performance reports.

🚨

Emergency Breakdown Cover

24/7 emergency call-out with a guaranteed 4-hour response time. Our vans carry a full range of spares to resolve most faults on the first visit.

📊

Remote Monitoring

IoT-enabled monitoring dashboards that track system performance in real time, alerting our engineers to potential issues before they become costly failures.

📋

Compliance & F-Gas Reporting

Mandatory F-Gas leak checks, refrigerant handling and complete compliance documentation to keep you on the right side of regulations.

🌿 Additional Services

🔍

System Surveys & Audits

Comprehensive energy audits and system surveys with actionable recommendations to reduce running costs and environmental impact.

♻️

Energy-Efficient Upgrades

Replace ageing R22 or R410A systems with modern R32 or heat-pump technology—eligible for government energy efficiency schemes.

💨

Ventilation & Air Quality

MVHR, heat recovery ventilation and indoor air quality solutions to ensure your building meets current building regulations and occupant wellbeing standards.
